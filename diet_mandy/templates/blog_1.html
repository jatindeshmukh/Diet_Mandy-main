Healthy Indian Breakfast Ideas

A nutritious breakfast boosts metabolism & energy for the day.

Oats Upma

Fiber Rich Low Calorie Heart Healthy

A modern, healthier take on the traditional South Indian upma, made with fiber-rich oats, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices. This dish provides sustained energy release while being gentle on your digestive system.

Moong Dal Chilla

Protein Packed Gluten Free Easy Digestion

Savory, crepe-like pancakes made from ground moong dal (split yellow lentils). These are a fantastic source of plant-based protein and make for a light yet satisfying breakfast option.

Vegetable Poha

Light & Nutritious Quick to Prepare Iron Rich

Flattened rice cooked with vegetables, peanuts, and mild spices - a popular Western Indian breakfast that's light on the stomach yet packed with nutrients and flavor.
