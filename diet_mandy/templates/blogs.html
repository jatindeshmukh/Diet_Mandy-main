Balanced Diet vs Trendy Diets

Crash diets give quick results but harm your metabolism. A balanced diet fuels your body sustainably.

Balanced Diet Includes

  • Protein (Dal, Eggs, Paneer, Chicken)
  • Good carbs (Millets, Rice, Roti)
  • Healthy fats (Ghee, Nuts)
  • Fruits & vegetables
  • Plenty of water

Portion Control Guide

Portion control prevents overeating & supports healthy weight loss without strict diets.

Tips

  • ½ plate veggies, ¼ carbs, ¼ protein
  • Use smaller plates
  • Eat slowly & mindfully
  • Stop at 80% fullness
