Vegetarian Protein Sources

Vegetarians can easily fulfill protein needs with smart food choices and proper meal planning.

Lentils & Dal

High Protein | Rich in Iron

Lentils are nutritional powerhouses packed with protein, fiber, and essential minerals. They are versatile and can be used in various dishes from soups to curries.

Popular Varieties:

Masoor Dal Moong Dal Toor Dal Chana Dal Urad Dal
18g Protein per 100g
7g Fiber
350 Calories

Chickpeas & Beans

Complete Protein | High Fiber

Legumes like chickpeas and various beans provide excellent protein along with complex carbohydrates. They help in maintaining stable blood sugar levels.

Common Types:

Chickpeas Kidney Beans Black Beans Soybeans Lima Beans
19g Protein per 100g
8g Fiber
360 Calories

Paneer & Curd

Calcium Rich | Easy Digestion

Dairy products are excellent sources of complete protein and calcium. Paneer provides sustained energy while curd offers probiotics for gut health.

Dairy Options:

Paneer Greek Yogurt Milk Buttermilk Cheese
18g Protein per 100g
200mg Calcium
280 Calories

Sprouts & Soya Chunks

Nutrient Dense | High Bioavailability

Sprouting increases protein content and digestibility. Soya products are complete proteins containing all essential amino acids for optimal nutrition.

Powerful Sources:

Moong Sprouts Soya Chunks Tofu Tempeh Edamame
52g Protein per 100g
13g Fiber
330 Calories

Nuts & Seeds

Healthy Fats | Energy Boost

Nuts and seeds provide protein along with healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. They are perfect for snacking and adding crunch to meals.

Nutritious Options:

Almonds Peanuts Walnuts Flaxseeds Chia Seeds Pumpkin Seeds
21g Protein per 100g
12g Healthy Fats
575 Calories

Quinoa & Ancient Grains

Complete Protein | Gluten Free

Quinoa is one of the few plant foods that contains all nine essential amino acids, making it a complete protein source ideal for vegetarians.

Grain Options:

Quinoa Amaranth Buckwheat Millets Oats
14g Protein per 100g
7g Fiber
370 Calories

Remember: A well-planned vegetarian diet can provide all the protein your body needs for optimal health and fitness.
