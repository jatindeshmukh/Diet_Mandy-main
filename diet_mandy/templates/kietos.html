Understanding the Keto Diet

The Ketogenic (Keto) diet is a low-carbohydrate, high-fat eating plan that aims to shift your body's metabolism into a state called ketosis. In ketosis, your body burns stored fat for fuel instead of glucose (sugar), helping with faster fat loss and improved energy levels. The goal is to limit carb intake to around 20-50 grams per day while increasing fats and maintaining moderate protein levels.
